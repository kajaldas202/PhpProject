Our Curriculum

Kider’s award-winning Seven Petal Preschool Curriculum aims at providing children with a solid developmental foundation by implementing a multi-sensory integrated approach to education and learning.

Kider's proprietary ‘Seven-Petal’ Preschool Curriculum, developed exclusively for 2-6 year olds, ensures holistic development of every child by following Sequential Learning and Developmental Milestones. The curriculum uses an Eclectic Approach model to subsequently improve learning effectiveness in children.
