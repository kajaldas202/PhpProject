Admission Eligibility

  • Kidzo Junior: 4 to 5 years
  • Kidzo Senior: 5 to 6 years
  • Day Care: 9 months to 10 years

Admission Procedure

  • Submission of Enquiry Form:
  • Interaction with the Center Head
  • Confirmation of Admission
  • Admission Form Collection
  • Payment of the Confirmation Fee and Form Submission

Documents

  • Completed Application Form
  • Certified true copy of the child's birth certificate issued by the relevant Municipal Corporation or Passport copy for date of birth proof
  • Five passport size colour photographs of the child
  • Three stamp size colour photographs of the child
  • Three passport size colour photographs of father and mother along with ld proof and Address proof.

Here are answers to your questions

Admission FAQs How can

What age groups does Kidzonia International Preschool and DayCare cater to?
Kidzonia welcomes children from 9 months to 10 years old, offering specialised programs for different age groups within this range.
What makes Kidzonia different from other preschools and daycare centres?
Kidzonia stands out as the ‘School of Experiences’. We offer a unique blend of education and play, focusing on experiential learning, fostering creativity, and nurturing social skills. Our daycare facilities are designed to provide a secure, stimulating, and enriching environment.
What is the admissions process like?
To enrol your child, begin by filling out our online application form on the Kidzonia website. After receiving your application, we will contact you to schedule a school tour and an interactive session with our admissions team.
Is there a specific curriculum followed at Kidzonia?
Yes, we follow a thematic and experiential learning-based curriculum that encourages children to explore, imagine, and apply their knowledge. Our programs are designed to enhance cognitive, physical, and social development.
What are the daycare operating hours?
Our daycare services are available from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. to accommodate the varying schedules of working parents. We offer flexible options to suit your needs.
What security measures are in place for the safety of the children?
Kidzonia prioritises the safety of every child. We have CCTV cameras installed throughout the premises, and parents have access to live feeds. Our staff is well-trained in emergency procedures, and the daycare area is equipped with child-friendly features and secure sleeping arrangements.
Are meals provided for the children?
Yes, we provide nutritious and freshly prepared meals onsite, ensuring that your child’s dietary needs are met. Pediatricians and nutritionists have created our food menus to promote healthy eating habits.
How do parents stay updated on their child's progress?
We maintain open communication with parents. Regular updates, including daily activities, achievements, and upcoming events, are shared through our dedicated app. Parent-teacher meetings are also scheduled periodically to discuss your child’s development.
Is transportation provided by Kidzonia?
We do not provide transportation services. However, our location is conveniently accessible, and we can assist parents in finding reliable transportation options if needed.
What extracurricular activities are offered at Kidzonia?
We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities, including music, dance, art, and sports, tailored to enhance creativity and physical development. These activities are integrated into our curriculum to provide a holistic learning experience.
What does Kidzonia mean by the ‘School of Experiences’?
Kidzonia believes that every child’s learning journey should be filled with meaningful experiences. The ‘School of Experiences’ signifies our commitment to providing a learning environment where children engage in hands-on activities, explore their creativity, and develop essential life skills through real-life situations, making their learning experiences memorable and impactful.
Why is play-based learning important at Kidzonia?
Play-based learning is crucial as it fosters a child’s curiosity, creativity, and problem-solving skills. Through play, children explore, experiment, and make sense of the world around them. At Kidzonia, play-based learning forms the foundation of our teaching approach, ensuring that education is enjoyable, interactive, and tailored to each child’s unique learning style.
How does Kidzonia's DISCOVER curriculum emphasise experiential learning?
Kidzonia’s DISCOVER curriculum is designed to prioritise experiential learning. By immersing children in hands-on activities, field trips, storytelling sessions, and interactive projects, we encourage them to explore, question, and learn through their senses. This approach allows children to grasp complex concepts in a tangible way, making learning more engaging and memorable. Experiential learning in our curriculum ensures that children actively participate in their education, leading to a deeper understanding of the subjects and fostering a lifelong love for learning.
How can parents contribute to enhancing the Kidzonia experience?
Kidzonia’s program is structured to involve parents in a multitude of events and celebrations held throughout the year. Additionally, parents actively participate in meaningful dialogues about their child’s progress during our regular Parent-Teacher Meetings (PTM). These interactions foster a collaborative environment, ensuring parents are engaged and informed about their child’s development journey.
